Crowe National Case Competition

In the competition, my focus was in line with the "Guardians of Integrity," a group dedicated to preventing and detecting fraud. I investigated how artificial intelligence could be used to improve the management of fraud risks, and proposed inventive approaches and answers for organizations seeking to protect themselves from AI-related fraud risks, such as bribery, corruption, disbursements, expense management, third-party risk, and vendor fraud. My proposed and developed solutions revolved around Anomaly Detection, Predictive Analysis, Natural Language Processing, Real-time monitoring, and Behavior Analysis to identify fraudulent transactions or schemes that might evolve and alter the conventional definition and characteristics of fraud.
